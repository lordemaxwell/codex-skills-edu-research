# Relevance Rules

Use these heuristics when the user asks for highly relevant or “有启发性” literature.

## Direct Relevance

Prioritize papers that match at least one of these:
- the exact policy, intervention, or institutional setting
- the same population, region, school stage, or labor market segment
- the same core outcome variable
- the same identification or evaluation strategy

## Inspiration Value

Promote papers even if they are not exact-topic matches when they offer:
- a stronger empirical design
- a transferable mechanism explanation
- a new measurement strategy
- a useful review or synthesis of adjacent literature
- policy evidence from a comparable country or region

## Education and Economics Bias

Default priority areas:
- policy evaluation
- education finance and inequality
- human capital and labor returns
- regional development
- causal inference, quasi-experimental methods, and high-quality reviews

## Short Relevance Note Template

Use compact notes such as:
- `直接覆盖“双减”政策评估，并提供可借鉴的因果识别框架。`
- `主题相邻，但方法更强，适合借鉴其 DID 设计。`
- `不是教育主题主文献，但对财政分权与教育支出机制有启发。`
